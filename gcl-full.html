The Covenant Code

Legion Laws & Sacred Commandments
"Let all things be done decently and in order." — 1 Corinthians 14:40
📜
Part I · Organization Standards
Legion Rules of Conduct
I
Honorable Language
We ask that all members keep their language clean and respectful at all times. Profanity has no place within these ranks — our words reflect who we are and Whose we are.
II
Wholesome Conduct
Lewd, inappropriate, or sexually suggestive humor is not welcome here. We hold ourselves to a standard that creates a safe and dignified environment for every member.
III
Patience Above All
Patience is not just a virtue — it is a requirement. Every member deserves to be heard, taught, and treated with grace. We grow together, and that takes time.
IV
Respect the Chain of Command
Our structure exists for a reason. Please honor and follow the chain of command. Direction flows from leadership down, and that order keeps the Legion unified and effective.
V
No Gossip
Gossip tears communities apart. Speaking negatively about fellow members behind closed doors will not be tolerated. If you have concerns, bring them forward properly — we address things with integrity.
VI
Proper Channels for Complaints
Every complaint and grievance is valued and deserves to be heard. All such matters will be handled through a private, respectful staff sit-down. Public disputes do not serve anyone well — please trust the process.
VII
Final Authority
The Spiritual Leader and Org Leader hold final say in all matters. Their decisions are made with the Legion's best interests at heart. Please extend them the trust and respect their position carries.
VIII
No Unnecessary Drama
Conflicts are part of life — but they must be brought to the proper channels to be resolved. Drama that is stirred up and not submitted to leadership will not be permitted to fester within this community.
IX
Unresolved Conflict
If a conflict between members escalates beyond what leadership can mediate and resolution cannot be reached, both parties may be removed from the Legion. Unity comes before any individual dispute.
X
One Faith, One Foundation
The God Chosen Legion is built on the Christian faith. Out of respect for our foundation and purpose, discussions promoting other religions or teachings contrary to sound Christian doctrine are not permitted within our community.
XI
The Holy Spirit Leads
We believe the Holy Spirit guides us into all truth. In matters of spiritual discernment and major decisions, His leading holds the highest authority. We trust His judgment above our own reasoning.
XII
Protect the Uninformed
Never look down on or belittle someone simply because they don't know something yet. Everyone begins somewhere. It is our privilege to guide, teach, and lift up — not to mock or diminish.
XIII
Rank Is Responsibility
A title within this Legion is not a trophy to display — it is a calling to serve. Every rank comes with real responsibility and real work. Any misuse of authority or power for personal gain will be addressed swiftly.
XIV
Spiritual Disciplines Encouraged
Reading scripture and prayer are not mandatory, but they are deeply encouraged. These disciplines strengthen who we are as individuals and as a community. We welcome every step of growth, however small.
XV
Activity Is Required
To maintain a healthy, functioning organization both in Discord and in-game, regular activity is required of all members. An active presence ensures we remain structured, connected, and ready in the field.
XVI
Ranks Are Earned
Advancement in rank is based on demonstrated competency and the genuine need of the Legion — not on time served, popularity, or personal relationships. Earn your place through character and capability.
XVII
Division Sovereignty
Each division operates within its own lane and handles its own matters independently. Divisions are not to be overstepped, bypassed, or interfered with outside of proper authority. Respect the structure that has been set in place.

Part II · The Church's Rules
The Ten Commandments
I
You shall have no other gods before Me.
II
You shall not make for yourself any idol, nor bow down to or worship it in any form.
III
You shall not take the name of the Lord your God in vain.
IV
Remember the Sabbath day, to keep it holy. Six days you shall labor, but the seventh is set apart for rest and worship.
V
Honor your father and your mother, that your days may be long upon the land the Lord your God is giving you.
VI
You shall not murder.
VII
You shall not commit adultery.
VIII
You shall not steal.
IX
You shall not bear false witness against your neighbor.
X
You shall not covet your neighbor's house, spouse, servants, or anything that belongs to your neighbor.
